Birthday Calculator

This calculator picks up the current date automatically from your machine or mobile phone and asks you to enter your birth date. Click on the submit button after entering your birth day and it will automatically determine your exact age in years, months, & days today. If you want to know how old you were when something happened in the past, just enter the event date at the top of the calculator and it will determine how old you were then.
